The Platform Approach

The Foundation of Voice Interfaces

Tambourine isn't a locked-down app. It's infrastructure you can build on to create exactly the voice experience you need.

Choose Your Models

Pick the STT and LLM that work for you. Use cloud providers or run fully local with Whisper and Ollama.

Write Your Prompts

Customize how your speech gets formatted. Add your own rules, adjust the tone, define how punctuation works.

Build Your Features

Implement features like backtracking corrections and list formatting your way. The logic is in prompts you control.

What You Can Build

Examples of Personalization

These features ship by default, but they're implemented in customizable prompts. Modify them, extend them, or build your own.

Personal Dictionary

Teach it technical terms, names, and jargon specific to your work.

"ant row pic" → Anthropic

Backtrack Corrections

Say "actually" or "scratch that" to correct mistakes as you speak.

"at 2 actually 3" → at 3

Smart List Formatting

Automatically format lists.

"one eggs two milk" → 1. Eggs 2. Milk

Your Own Ideas

The prompts and logic are yours to modify. Build what you need.

Edit prompts in Settings → Prompts
